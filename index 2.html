WHO AM I?

For starters, my name is Czar Paolo Capacite, 21 years of age, living in Pamacsalan, Pilar, Bohol. I’m not very much of interactive type of person especially in front of a stranger since I’m no expert in starting a conversation let alone bring up any interesting topics along the way, I tend to easily get attached to people I get to know more though. My guiding principles is simple, “just trust the process”. If you think you work hard on something and you get everything up, just trust what the outcome will be either it is a good or bad outcome, since if you succeed, your hard-work has finally paid off, and if it’s a bad outcome, you can use that mistake you made to grow better as a person. My accomplishments may be few, but I cherish most of them since because of that accomplishment, I get to know who I really am, and let’s start when I became the first honored student when I was in elementary school in Eastern Samar twice, I learned how to play guitar when I was in my tenth grade, we won the first place in our scavenger hunt contest. My dream is simple, just for me and for my family to live a normal happy life, and to accomplish that, I must hang on to my current course until graduation. .
